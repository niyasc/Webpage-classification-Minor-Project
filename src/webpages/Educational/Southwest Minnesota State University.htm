Almer Honored With MnSCU Award

Posted 10.10.2013

 Southwest Minnesota State University Human Resources Director Deb Almer was recently presented a Human Resources All-Star Award from the Minnesota State Colleges and Universities (MnSCU) system.

The award was presented at the MnSCU Human Resources Conference Sept. 24-25 in Alexandria.

Almer has been at SMSU for 25 years. She was one of two HR individuals recognized at the conference, attended by approximately 130 HR professions within the MnSCU system.
Almer was nominated for the award by SMSU President Connie J. Gores. Almer’s dedication to her job, leadership to the department and assistance to other MnSCU institutions were cited in her nomination.

Chef Franco Lania to Speak to Culinology Students

Posted 10.08.2013

 Chef Franco Lania will give a demonstration and lecture to SMSU Culinology students on Oct. 15 when he is in Marshall for the upcoming Fall & Holiday Cooking Show.


Chef Franco is a member of the Culinary Advisory Board for ConAgra Foods and has extensive experience in many highly-acclaimed restaurants in the U.S. and Europe. He was also featured on the television show “Jersey Gems,” which completed its season this past spring.

Van Drehle Named Director of Alumni Relations

Posted 09.27.2013

 Southwest Minnesota State University alumnus Mike Van Drehle has been named the permanent Director of Alumni Relations at Southwest Minnesota State University.

He began his duties on Sept. 21. He had been the interim director since January 2012.

Van Drehle earned Business Management and Marketing degrees in 2008 and later, a master’s in Business Administration (MBA) degree with a leadership concentration in 2010.

Van Drehle worked as a graduate assistant in the Alumni Relations Office while pursuing his MBA degree. He then accepted a position as an admission counselor at SMSU before being appointed the interim director.
