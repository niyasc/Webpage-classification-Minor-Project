What We Believe

AUM Values and Promotes a Standard of Excellence.



"Excellence is a standard I expect my students to reach for here at AUM, a standard I expect I should strive for always in my teaching, administration, and scholarship; achieving excellence, though, is impossible unless it's the goal, even if sometimes, our reach exceeds our grasp. I am always inspired by the words of Robert Browning: 'Ah, but a man's reach should exceed his grasp, or what's a heaven for?' "

Dr. E.D. Woodworth
Associate Professor of English
