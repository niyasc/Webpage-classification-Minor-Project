Community

Find your place.
Grow in community.

Bethel is all about community. That means making friends and memories to last a lifetime. But it’s about more than that. Here, we challenge each other just as much as we support each other.

Our students are gracious servants, compassionate friends, and thoughtful neighbors who are intentional about helping each other grow, and having a lot of fun while they’re at it.

Faith

Live out your faith. Spread Christ’s love.

Here, we take everything we believe and infuse it into everything we do. It’s not just something we talk about. We work hard to understand Christ’s message and try our best to spread his love, every day.

We know this isn’t easy. It asks a lot from each one of us. That’s why we don’t work at it alone. Our community is full of people to support you as you build a personal Christian foundation and challenge you to serve with compassion, humility, and grace.

Prospective Students

Welcome. Our admissions team is here to serve you.

Meet our friendly admissions team, visit campus, and ask us questions. Get to know Bethel for yourself. Where do you fit?

Undergrad

For recent high school grads and transfer students looking for a liberal arts education within a vibrant Christian community.

Adult Undergrad

For working adults of all ages, experience levels, and backgrounds who want to complete their undergraduate degree or start their college education.

Graduate

For professionals interested in earning advanced degrees to move forward in their careers and grow as leaders.

Seminary

For men and women, of all ages, who sense a calling to ministry—youth, urban, rural, cross-cultural, and a growing range of unconventional settings.

About Bethel

Bethel University is a Christian college in Minnesota serving undergraduate, graduate, and seminary students. We believe a college education is about discovering your God-given strengths and where you're called to serve.
