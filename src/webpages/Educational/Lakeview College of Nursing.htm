With a nursing history of excellence ...

Lakeview College of Nursing has a proud tradition of preparing nurses for professional practice for over 100 years. Additionally, the College is committed to our values of: Adaptability, Caring, Excellence, Integrity and Service. We take pride ... Learn more »

Where nurses are born ...

Lakeview College of Nursing is a single purpose institution that has focused on excellence in nursing education since 1894. The Bachelor of Science in Nursing Program is offered in Danville, IL, as well as in Charleston, IL, through a cooperative agreement with Eastern Illinois University. Learn more »
