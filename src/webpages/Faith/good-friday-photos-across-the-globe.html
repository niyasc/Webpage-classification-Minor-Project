Good Friday photos from across the globe
Friday, March 29th, 2013

Good Friday photos from across the globe; Christians mark Easter, Holy Week.

1

Sri Lankan Christian devotees take part in Way of the Cross rituals which symbolize the journey of Jesus Christ before he was crucified, during a Good Friday service in Colombo March 29, 2013. Christians account for around six percent of Sri Lanka's 20 million population.

(LAKRUWAN WANNIARACHCHI - AFP/Getty Images)
2

A homeless man, has his beard trimmed by Sister Christobel Fonseca in Mumbai on Good Friday, March 29, 2013. Volunteers from Mother Teresa's Roses, armed with cleaning and medical supplies, find destitute people on the streets and bathe them, clean their wounds and give them a haircut.

(INDRANIL MUKHERJEE - AFP/Getty Images)
3

Penitents carry a statue of a dead Christ dated 1728 during a procession for Good Friday on the southern island of Procida in Naples on March 29, 2013. The 'Misteri floats,' depicting scenes from the Bible, are carried in the streets during the annual procession which started in 1627.

(MARIO LAPORTA - AFP/Getty Images)
4

Indonesian Catholics participate in a re-enactment of the crucifixion of Jesus Christ on Good Friday on March 29, 2013 in Magelang, Central Java, Indonesia. Catholics make up approximately 3 per cent of the predominantly Muslim country.

(Ulet Ifansasti - AFP/Getty Images)
5

An American pilgrim from 'The Hope of Glory' order reenacts the Passion of Christ along the Via Dolorosa during the Good Friday procession on on March 29, 2013 in Jerusalem's Old City, Israel.

(Uriel Sinai - AFP/Getty Images)
6

Penitents carry a sculpture of the crucifixion during a procession for Good Friday on the southern island of Procida in Naples on March 29, 2013.

(MARIO LAPORTA - AFP/Getty Images)
7

Paris' archbishop Andre Vingt-Trois walks past a nun talking on a walkie-talkie as he takes part in a Way of the Cross to celebrate Good Friday, in front of the Sacre-Coeur Basilica in Montmartre district, in Paris, on March 29, 2013.

(JOEL SAGET - AFP/Getty Images)
8

A Christian worshiper prays at the Church of the Holy Sepulchre during the Good Friday on March 29, 2013 in Jerusalem's Old City, Israel. Good Friday is celebrated by Christians throughout the world as the day Christ was crucified on the cross in the lead up to his resurrection on Easter.

(Uriel Sinai - AFP/Getty Images)
9

Pakistani Christian devotees gather around a Crucifix as they attend a Good Friday service at the Saint Francis Church in Lahore on March 29, 2013. Christian believers around the world mark the Holy Week of Easter in celebration of the crucifixion and resurrection of Jesus Christ.

(ARIF ALI - AFP/Getty Images)
10

Actor James Burke-Dunsmore plays Jesus in 'The Passion of Jesus' on Good Friday to crowds in Trafalgar Square on March 29, 2013 in London, England. 'The Passion of Jesus' includes a cast of 78 actors, horses, a donkey and authentic costumes of Roman soldiers in the 12th Legion of the Roman Army.

(Oli Scarff - AFP/Getty Images)
11

Actor James Burke-Dunsmore plays Jesus in 'The Passion of Jesus' on Good Friday to crowds in Trafalgar Square on March 29, 2013 in London, England. The Wintershall Players are based on the Wintershall Estate in Surrey and perform several biblical theatrical productions per year.

(Oli Scarff - AFP/Getty Images)
12

Penitents take part in the procession of the "Road to Calvary," also known as the "Turbas," during the Holy Week in Cuenca on March 29, 2013. The procession was canceled due to bad weather. Christian believers around the world mark the Holy Week of Easter in celebration of the crucifixion and resurrection of Jesus Christ.

(PEDRO ARMESTRE - AFP/Getty Images)
13

Paloma Martin comforts her son, Cesar Higuero Martin, 25, after he had walked the way of the cross or 'Via Crucis' at the end of the procession of the 'Empalaos' on March 29, 2013 in Valverde de la Vera, Spain.

(David Ramos - AFP/Getty Images)
14

Believers gather around the Macarena Virgin (Our Lady of Hope) during a Holy Week procession in Sevilla on March 29, 2013. Christian believers around the world mark the Holy Week of Easter in celebration of the crucifixion and resurrection of Jesus Christ.

(CRISTINA QUICLER - AFP/Getty Images)
