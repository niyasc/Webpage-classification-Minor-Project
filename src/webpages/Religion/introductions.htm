Bibliography & Bookstore: Introductions to Islam

  • Islam Today: A Short Introduction to the Muslim World
    Akbar Ahmed
  • Islam for Dummies
    Malcolm Clark
  • The Complete Idiot's Guide(R) to Understanding Islam
    Yahiya Emerick and Qasim Najar (Alpha Books, 2001).
  • What Everyone Needs to Know About Islam
    John Esposito
  • An Introduction to Islam
    Frank Mathewson Denny, 2nd ed.
  • An Introduction to Islam
    Gerhard Endress (English trans. from German, 1994)
  • The Venture of Islam
    Marshall G.S. Hodgson, 3 vol. (1974). An influential study of Islamic religion and civilization.
  • Understanding Islam: An Introduction to the Muslim World
    Thomas W. Lippman, 3rd ed.
  • The Muslim Almanac: A Reference Work on the History, Faith, Culture, and Peoples of Islam
    Azim A Nanji (ed.) (1996)
  • A Popular Dictionary of Islam
    I.R. Netton (London: Curzon Press, 1997).
  • Islam: A Very Short Introduction
    Malise Ruthvan
  • Introduction to Islamic Civilization
    R.M. Savory (ed.), (1976). Collection of scholarly articles on Islamic history, religion, literature, language, and other topics.
  • Islam: Religion, History, and Civilization
    Seyyed Hossein Nasr
  • Islam
    Fazlur Rahman (Chicago: Chicago UP, 1979).
  • Islam: An Introduction
    Annemarie Schimmel
  • Islam: A Concise Introduction
    Huston Smith
